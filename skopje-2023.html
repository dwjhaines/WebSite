Skopje 2023

North Macedonia v England

20th November 2023

Friday 17th November We caught the 1pm bus from Kingsclere to Basingstoke and then the 13:49 train to Reading. We were not in any hurry to get to Heathrow so went to the Wetherspoons for lunch and a few drinks. We walked back to the station and had a few more drinks in The Three Guineas before getting an Elizabeth line train to West Drayton at 6:30. We had one more drink in the pub by the station, bought food and a couple of bottles of wine and caught a bus to The Thistle Hotel near Terminal 5.

We were not near a great selection of pubs so drank the wine in the room before going to bed.
