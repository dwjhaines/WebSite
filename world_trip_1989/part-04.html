Saturday, 4th March The flight was quite good with plenty of free drink. We arrived at about 6:30 Sydney Time and after clearing customs, we caught a bus into Sydney centre. From there we got a taxi to Bondi where we hoped to stay with Dai Hughes and Clair (Ex Clynians whose address we had been given by Liz). We hadn't warned them that we were coming but luckily they were in and we were told to stay as long as we wanted. We were also fortunate in that they were having a party tonight and we all ended up getting pissed. At 3am I phoned Gareth to find out the rugby results.
