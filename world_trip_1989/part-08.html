Part 8 - Sydney

  • Diary Index

Friday, 15th September We spent the day looking for a house. We drove out to Bondi where we wanted to find a place and spent about two hours walking round estate agents. We started off looking for a four or five bedroom house but soon realised that this would be hard to find and expensive. We eventually settled for a two bedroom flat which is about a ten minute walk form the beach. The flat is unfurnished and a bit small for five people but it's cheap ($180 per week) and ideally situated. By the time we had sorted out gas, electricity, etc it was about 4pm. We drove back to the Y.H.A. where we were booked in for another night.

Me, Ed and Gordon went out for a curry.

Saturday, 16th September We moved into our flat in the morning. I have the "sun room" to myself. It is only about 8' by 5' but at least it is my own room. We spent most of the day sorting our things out and constructing furniture out of cardboard boxes. We had to buy a fridge and a few mattresses from a second hand shop and also bought some plaltes, etc from a garage sale.

In the evening we all went out to Kings Cross, Sydney's red light district. We managed to buy some grass and then returned to the flat.

Sunday, 17th September We had planned to go sight-seeing today but no-one could be bothered. It was too cold to go to the beach so we spent the afternoon drinking beer and playing Trivial Pursuit. Started growing pot plants.

Monday, 18th September We all started looking for work. I tried for a job from the paper on my way to the city but was too late. Once in the city, I got a job with the first agency I went to. It is clerical work on the far side of the city for $10.62 an hour. It is not ideal as it is quite a way from Bondi but it will do until I find something better. Jane also got a job as a kitchen hand/waitress. Linda has a job as a housekeeper.

Tuesday, 19th September My job is pretty boring. It is typical office work, filing, data entry, drinking tea, etc. It takes an hour each way in the car.

Wednesday, 20th September The job got a bit more interesting. In the evening we went out and hired a television. It is brand new and only costs $20 a month.

Thursday, 21st September I discovered that the company I am working for is the one that owns Jewsons. I spent most of the day putting invoices into envelopes. I am getting fed up with having to drive to work each day. The job would be quite good if it was in the city.

Friday, 22nd September I had a day off work today as they only wanted me for three days this week. Jane and I went into Sydney and went round the sights. We saw the opera house and bridge and spent about an hour in the harbour area. We had lunch at Martin Place where bands perform every day. I collected my pay check from work and we went back to Bondi.

Saturday, 23rd September We went to a garage sale in the morning and managed to buy a set of saucepans so we can start cooking food at last. We didn't do much all day. The weather is not really hot enough for lying on the beach all day.

Spent the evening watching tele. I can't afford to go out at the moment as all my pay is going on debts built up over the last month.

Sunday, 24th September Spent a couple of hours in the sun.

Monday, 25th - Friday, 29th September A pretty boring week at work. I decided to take the train instead of driving. This takes a bit longer but is much easier.

Jane and Linda both gave up their jobs during the week. I have only got a week to to with my job but I don't think I will bother turning up for it on Tuesday. It is so easy to find work here that I should find something better within a few days.

Saturday, 30th September I decided that I was definitely going to pack in my job. We spent a bit of time on the beach but it is not really hot enough for sun-bathing yet.

Sunday, 1st October We spent just about all day sitting in the lounge watching tele, playing cards and drinking beer.

Monday, 2nd October Federation Day. We decided to go sight-seeing. We got the train into Sydney and then got onto a boat from Circular Quay to Darlinig Harbour. We had lunch at Darling Harbour and then spent the rest of the afternoon in a nearby pub. In the evening, we went to a few more pubs and then went for a meal at a B.Y.O. restaurant in China Town. We returned to Bondi and spent a couple of hours in The Bondi Hotel.

Tuesday, 3rd - Friday, 6th October I didn't go in to work on Tuesday so I spent the rest of teh week signing on at various job agencies without much enthusiasm and no success.

On THursday, the council threatened to tow the car away as they thought it had been abandoned.

Saturday, 7th October We went out to a few pubs and a B.Y.O. restaurant in the evening.

Sunday, 8th October Everyone had hangovers so we didn't do much.

Monday, 9th - Friday, 13th October Another boring week looking for work. There is lots of work about but we are missing out on a lot of agency work because we are not on the phone. I had an interview with the police department on Friday for a job fitting radios into police cars. I just missed out on the job but have to ring back next week to see if there are any more vacancies. Jane and Linda both start work on Monday. They have packing jobs at The Body Shop.

We discovered during the week that Hugh has got fed up of Australia and has flown home.

Saturday, 14th October Went out for a few drinks and a curry

Sunday, 15th October A quiet day.

Monday, 16th October Spent the day job hunting again. It is frustrating not being able to find work when there seems to be so many jobs around. The C.E.S. wont give out most of their jobs to people on working visas and the job agencies don't seem much use as we don't have a phone. I am now relying heavily on getting the job with the police department. We had a postcard from Darwin from Roz. She is going to Adelaide and should get here in a few weeks. Dave has fallen in love again and is heading back to Perth.

Tuesday, 17th October I gave up job hunting at about 9:30 after a brief visit to Bondi Junction C.E.S. I had a letter from Paul at the post office saying he would be here in the next couple of days and then returned to the house to find him already there. He had bought a bag of grass with him so we got stoned and spent the afternoon in The Bondi Hotel.

Jane and Linda came back from work and told me I could have a job at the place where they work if I don't get the job with the police. Spent the rest of the day celebrating.

Wednesday, 18th October I phoned the police department at 9:00 and was told that I didn't get the job. I then went into Sydney for an interview at The Body Shop where Jane and Linda work. The interview lasted about five minutes and I was told straight away that I could start next Monday. The job looks pretty boring but there seems to be a good crowd of people working there.

Thursday, 19th October I didn't do much all day. Gordon went for an interview for a job as a security guard at teh post office in Sydney.

Friday, 20th October Another lazy day. It is quite good not having to look for work. Gordon got the job at the post office and started at 3pm. SPent the evening watching tele whilst drinking another case of VB.

Saturday, 21st October We went out and got drunk again (except Gordon who was working).

Sunday, 22nd October Didn't do much due to hangovers again.

Monday, 23rd October I started work at The Body Shop. The job is pretty boring. It involves packing gift packs of BOdy Shop products. The money is not too good but we are on a bonus scheme so it might improve if we increase productivity. I don't know how long I will stick it but I will certainly carry on until I find something better.

Tuesday, 24th October I spent seven and a half hours putting ribbons on gift packs. The job is drivinig me mad.

Went to The Bondi Hotel for a couple of drinks in the evening.

Wednesday, 25th October I got really fed up with the job today and started phoning the job agencies again at lunchtime. When I got home in the evening, there was a note telling me to contact the police department about the job I went for a couple of weeks ago. Apparently, the bloke they took on has left already so they now have a vacancy again.

Roz turned up in the evening so we went down to The Bondi Hotel and got drunk.

Thursday, 26th October I phone the police department on the way to work and was told that I could start work there on Monday. I went into The Body Shop and gave in my notice, intending to stay there for two more days but was told to leave straight away. This did not bother me a great deal!

All of us now have jobs. Ed and Paul both have surveying jobs, Jane and Linda are still at The Body Shop and Gordon is packing in his job at the post office and starts a new job next week.

Spent the afternoon walking round Sydney with Roz and Gordon (McDonalds and Harbour Bridge mainly). In the eveing, Jane, Roz and I went to The Coogee Bay Hotel with some people from work. We scored some grass and returned to the house to get stoned.

Friday, 27th October Roz and I spent most of the day sitting round the house getting stoned. In the evening we went into town and met up with Linda, Jane and Paul and a load of girls from The Body Shop. We had a few drinks at The Centrepoint Tavern and then returned to Bondi for a meal at a Cambodian restaurant. Needless to say, we ended up drunk again.

Saturday, 28th October I had a hangover for most of the day. It didn't really go until we started drinking again in the evening. We went out to a Thai restaurant in Bondi and then to The Bondi Hotel.

Sunday, 29th October A quiet day. In the evning, we cooked a roast dinner.

Monday, 30th October I started my fourteenth job since getting to Australia. The job would be quite interesting if there was more to do but apart from about ten minutes work, I spent the whole day standing around doing nothing. The money is ridiculous considering what I actually do - $426.80 for a 32½ hour week.

Tuesday, 31st October A better day. I was working for about half the time I was there.

Wednesday, 1st - Friday, 3rd November After a week, I have decided that the job is not too bad and I will probably keep it until I leave Sydney. On Friday, I finshed at 3:15 adn went into the city where I met Roz, Linda, Paul, Ed and Gordon. We were all quite pissed before leaving the pubb and we ended up at a Thai restaurant again. In the Bondi Hotel I met a girl who had worked on the same stall as me at the Darwin Show.

Saturday, 4th November Spent the day with a hangover. I played squash with Linda at lunchtime and then spent the rest of the afternoon on the beer again. We went out for a meal in the evening and then went to Coogee to see "The Men They COuldn't Hang". I was quite drunk by the time we got there and managed to sprain my ankle during the concert.

Sunday, 5th November Watched the Australian Grand Prix on the tele and had an early night.

Monday, 6th November I decided not to go to work as my ankle was still hirting. I went to the doctor and he has given me a certificate to stay off work for the whole week.

Tuesday, 7th and Wednesday, 8th November Sat around the house doing nothing.

Thursday, 9th November I'd become bored of sitting around in the house so went back to work. I got paid $600. In the evening, we watched The Sound of Music and went to The Bondi Hotel until 2:30.

Friday, 10th November Had a slight hangover at work and left at 2:30 as I was bored. We went to The Thai Flora for a meal and then me, Linda, Roz, Paul and Ed went to the Bondi Hotel where we met Jane. We stayed in the pub until closing and then Linda and I managed to end up leaving the pub with some bloke who invited us round for drinks. We eventually got back at around 5.

Saturday, 11th and Sunday, 12th November We woke at about midday and decided to start on the session straight away. We drove to Coogee for a drink (me, Linda, Roz and Ed) and stopped for a drink in Randwick on the way home. We spent what was left of the afternoon drinking in the flat. In the evening, Jane and Paul went to see The Ramones, Gordon went to work and Linda had an early night so me, Roz and Ed went to The Bondi Hotel and stayed until it shut. When we got home, Roz and I stayed up all night while Ed slept for a couple of hours. By about 6am, Ed had recovered and Linda joined us for a quick game of chase the ace.

This got us all drunk pretty quickly and at about 8, I can vaguely remember going to McDonalds in Bondi Junction for breakfast (except Roz who was unconscious). I eventually got to sleep at about 11:30am and slept for about five hours. In the evening we went out for a curry and had an early night.

Monday, 13th to Thursday, 16th November An uneventful week. Went to Coogee on Wednesday.
