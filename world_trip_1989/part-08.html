Part 8 - Sydney

  • Diary Index

Saturday, 15th September We spent the day looking for a house. We drove out to Bondi where we wanted to find a place and spent about two hours walking round estate agents. We started off looking for a four or five bedroom house but soon realised that this would be hard to find and expensive. We eventually settled for a two bedroom flat which is about a ten minute walk form the beach. The flat is unfurnished and a bit small for five people but it's cheap ($180 per week) and ideally situated. By the time we had sorted out gas, electricity, etc it was about 4pm. We drove back to the Y.H.A. where we were booked in for another night.

Me, Ed and Gordon went out for a curry.
