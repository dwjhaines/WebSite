Part 3 - New Zealand

  • Diary Index

Tuesday, 28th February We arrived in Auckland at 10:30 and hired a car for the four days we are in New Zealand. We spent the day driving around Auckland with Chris, someone from England who was at the Waikiki Youth Hostel and on our flight.

It felt good to be driving on the left again and in many respects, the city was very English. We drove up Mt Eden for a view of the town. At 5, we checked into a hostel for $12 each. We cooked sausages for tea and spent the evening drinking beer in the hostels garden.

Wednesday, 1st March We drove down to the town of Tawanga where we spent the day sight-seeing. There wasn't really much to see but what was there was quite good. We toured the Durham Light Kiwi Fruit Winery and after tasting the full range, bought a couple of bottles. We spent about an hour in a hot pool and then walked up Mt Maunganui which gave a great view of the area. We drove from there to Rotarua where we checked into another hostel for the night.
