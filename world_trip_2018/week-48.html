Monday, 15th April We arrived on time at Heathrow at about 05:30. We had a bit of a delay waiting for my luggage as it was one of the last things to appear but we were through the airport about an hour later. Aleks and Andi were waiting to meet us when we came through and drove us back to Farnborough. After breakfast, I collected my car and we drove back to Kingsclere.

Tuesday, 16th April Roz went into work to find out that she will be starting her job again on 1st July.

Wednesday, 17th April First bike ride of the year in the morning. We drove to Nottingham in the afternoon. Went out for a few drinks in the evening. Watched Man City v Spurs in the TBI.

Thursday, 18th April Visited Aunty Jean in the morning. I went out on my bike again from there and did another 18 miles. We drove to Harrogate after lunch and checked into The Cairn Hotel for two nights. After cycling out to a couple of pubs, we went for a Wetherspoons curry followed by a Cask Marque pub crawl.

Friday, 19th April 11:00 train from Harrogate to Newcastle via York. We had a few beers in Newcastle before getting another train down to Sunderland for the Sunderland v Doncaster game at 5:30. A few problems with late trains on the way back ending up with Northern Rail getting us a taxi from York to Harrogate as we had missed our connection. Back in Harrogate just before midnight.

Saturday, 20th April Eleven mile bike ride along The Nidderdale Greenway before checking out of the hotel and driving back to Kingsclere. Out on our bikes again in the evening followed by a few drinks in the house and a trip down to The Swan.

Sunday, 21st April Another 18 miles on the bike followed by a bar-b-q at Louise's.
