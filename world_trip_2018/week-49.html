Monday, 22nd April A recovery day. Did not do much apart from another 13 miles on the bikes in the evening.

Tuesday, 23rd April Car MOT'd and serviced. Shopping in Newbury in the morning. 27 miles on the bike in the afternoon.

Wednesday, 24th April Another 17 miles on the bike. Check-up at the dentist. Drove to Llansteffan. Drank too much wine!

Thursday, 25th April A trip into Carmarthen hospital followed by a walk to look at Laugharne. Ate out at the pub in Llanybri

Friday, 26th April My birthday. Drove to Swansea and started drinking at 5pm. Roz, Cathy and I walked to the Potter's Wheel where we met up with Dave. We got a taxi to The Pilot in Mumbles at about 7 and were joined by Julie. We were in there a couple of hours before walking to The Park via The Rugby Club. Vaguely remember going for a curry before getting a taxi back to Cathy's.

Saturday, 27th April Swansea v Hull followed by a drive back to Kingsclere. Pizzas and presents followed by a few hours in The Swan.

Sunday, 28th April A day spent packing, sorting out the car for tomorrow, etc.
