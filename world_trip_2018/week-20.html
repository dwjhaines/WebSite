Monday, 1st October Drove from Nottingham to Newcastle to visit Audrey. Stayed at the Travelodge on the A1

Tuesday, 2nd October Roz stayed in Newcastle a second night. She spent a second day with Audrey and went to watch Sunderland v Peterborough. I got the train from Newcastle to Wigan for the Wigan v Swansea game. Met up with Eggi and a few others before the game. Stayed at the Wetherspoons about a mile from the centre of town

Wednesday, 3rd October I got the train from Wigan to Warrington where Roz picked me up on her way from Newcastle to South Wales. We drove down through mid Wales and had more enjoyable and probably quicker drive than using the motorway. Evening in Llansteffan.

Thursday, 4th October Went to Swansea for the Eli Jenkins quiz with Cathy and Dave(which we won!).

Friday, 5th October Another night in Llansteffan.

Saturday, 6th October Back to Swansea for the Swansea v Ipswich game. Lunch in The Potter's Wheel followed by a taxi to the game. Entertaining game despite the result! Evening spent in No Sign, Potter's Wheel and Anarkali but had a relatively early night.

Sunday, 7th October Drove back to Kingsclere. Twenty miles on my bike before going round to Louise's for a curry and a few drinks.
