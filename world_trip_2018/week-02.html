Monday, 28th May A very similar day to yesterday. After breakfast at the campsite, we went off on our bikes again. We followed the Altmühltal Radweg again but in the opposite direction this time. Roz did not want to cycle as far today so turned round at Kottingwörth but still did a round trip of 30 miles. I was quite keen on trying to get to Kelhiem at the end of the cycle route. This would have been a round trip of 80 miles. However, I gave up on this idea at Riedenburg. The cycling was relatively easy on flat paths and I should have been able to get there and back, but the 28° heat was taking its toll. Although the legs were fine, I decided the extra 20 miles was not a good idea particularly as a lot of the paths were unsurfaced making it harder work than normal. I turned round after 30 miles and just about made it back to Kipfenberg. Rest of the afternoon was spent recovering in the shade.
