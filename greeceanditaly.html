dwjhaines - greece and italy 2022

Wednesday 7th September We left Kingsclere at 9:30. We had booked a taxi for 9:45 but it arrived early which meant that we could get the 9:58 train from Basingstoke. Despite it being the Jubilee weekend, the trains were not as busy as expected and we were in Waterloo on time 45 minutes or so later. We got the tube to St Pancras where we arrived just after 11. Our train was not due to depart until 13:31 but we went to the Eurostar terminal first to check the queues. There has been a lot of disruption in the last week or so but the whole place seemed fairly quiet with no queuing at all meaning we had time to go to the Wetherspoons for our first beer of the trip.
