Monday 21st November - England v Iran A good start to the tournament. We met Dave and Hoggy in The Catherine Wheel at 12:30 and had reasonable seats to watch the first game. A convincing win and the start of an all day session. We went to The King Charles Tavern after the game for a couple and saw the first half of Netherlands v Senegal. We watched the second half in The Elephant on the Market before heading to The Hatchet.

We had curries in here before watching the Wales v USA game. They had not been showing the football earlier but were showing this game so there was not much point in moving. We were all getting quite pissed by this stage and only vaguely remember the taxi ride back to Kingsclere and another pint in The Swan.

Friday 25th November - England v USA We decided not to go out for this one. The pubs of Newbury were likely to be packed not only because of the football but it is Gold Cup day tomorrow. There were not too many options for watching it in Kingsclere so we stayed at home with a few beers before going down the pub. Very disappointing performance but should just about guarantee qualification
