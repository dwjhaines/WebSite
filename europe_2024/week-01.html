Wednesday 29th May We were up at 4:30 and left Kingsclere at exactly 5:00. There were a few delays due to roadworks and the particularly unnecessary "Operation Stack" on the M20 but the journey was not too bad and we arrived at Dover before 7:30. The queues to get through French immigration were not too bad and we had checked in for the 8:25 P&O ferry to Calais by about 7:40. We had been told that there was a delay of about 20 minutes. We started boarding at 8:25 and did leave about 20 minutes later. We had breakfast on the ferry and arrived in Calais at about 11:30. By midday, we were on the motorway heading towards Belgium and Germany.
