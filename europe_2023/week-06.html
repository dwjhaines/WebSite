Thursday 6th July We had a late breakfast upstairs on the terrace but did not really do much else for the rest of the morning. At 11:30, Roz went up to lie on the terrace while I went for a walk to the ticket office. I was not really expecting much success here and sure enough, it was completely closed with no indication of if/when it is going to re-open.

I did meet Phil again. He is staying over the road so is able to keep checking a bit more easily than us. We exchanged email addresses and I walked back to the hotel again. Quite an enjoyable walk despite the lack of tickets. Batumi has definitely grown on me since our first walk along the sea-front.

After I had recovered from the walk (it is very hot here again) we went out to buy khachapuris for lunch and ate them on the terrace.
