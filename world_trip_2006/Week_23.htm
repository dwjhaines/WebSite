World Trip 2006/7 - Week 23

Monday, 13th November 2006 We had breakfast at 7 and left the campsite at 7:45. The site had been fine for a couple of nights. We did eventually find a shower we could use and the setting had been fantastic.

We had to get to the park exit by 9:30 to join the convoy heading for Tsavo West N.P. and did a game drive along the way. This was excellent! We saw a family of lions with three cubs and 6 - 7 others in the background. They were all quite a way from us but we had very good views of them anyway. We saw a carcass near the lions being devoured by vultures and returned to yesterday's buffalo to see the last of the meat being picked off by hyenas.

It took a couple of hours to reach Tsavo West. As we approached the park, we saw lots of giraffes close to the road. We stopped for pictures of the Shetani Lava Flow and got to the park gate at about 11:40.

We were driven to our lodge which was complete luxury compared to our accommodation for the last two nights. We had a really nice room with hot showers, electricity and a huge double bed. We had lunch in the restaurant which had really good views of the surrounding hills and a large watering hole. After lunch we spent an hour by the pool before the afternoon game drive.

The game drive was no-where near as good as the previous ones we have done. We were taken to a pool with lots of submerged hippos which was not too exciting and did not see too much else. The animals are harder to see at Tsavo because of the amount of trees and bushes and there did not seem to be many around (except plenty of dik-diks). By 6:30 we had given up and returned to the lodge.

We had a beer while watching the first animals going to the watering hole - mainly gazelles. After showers, we returned to the restaurant for dinner - an excellent five course meal. By the end of the meal, we had moved to a table at the front with a better view of the watering hole. We stayed for a couple more beers while watching zebras. It was another superb experience and a very nice way to end our last full day in Africa.

Tuesday, 14th November 2006 We were up by 6 for a 6:30 game drive. This turned out to be similar to yesterday's. There were a few animals we had not seen before and plenty of dik-diks again but it was not too exciting.

Back at the lodge we had an excellent breakfast overlooking the watering hole again. We had an hour in our room before checking out of the lodge and beginning the drive back to Nairobi. The first hour was through the park before we came to the main Mombassa - Nairobi road. It took a total of about six hours to get back. The Germans got dropped off at the airport. We continued back to the city centre to collect our $50 refund from Best Camping. We went out to buy a few last minute souvenirs/presents and spent some time on the internet before getting driven back to the airport by Peter.

We were at the airport just after 6 and were able to check-in almost immediately despite the fact that our flight was not due to depart until 23:20.

We wandered round a few of the souvenir shops getting rid of most of our remaining shillings and then sat in a bar getting rid of the rest. Quite light-headed when we eventually got on the plane. We took off at midnight and got quite pissed on wine before falling asleep.

Wednesday, 15th November 2006 We landed on time at 5am, got our luggage quickly and were back at the car by 6. We had five hours to kill before our flight to Amsterdam so decided to drive to Farnborough to collect the laptop. We were back at Heathrow by 9 and departed for Amsterdam just after 11. We were in Schipol by 1ish and got the train into the centre. We met Mark at the hotel and had a beer in the hotel bar while waiting for Steve who joined us shortly after we arrived.

We spent the remainder of the afternoon in De Wildemaan. We had a very enjoyable few hours in there before heading to the ground at 7. We made it into our seats just before kick-off with no Zagreb style dramas at the turnstiles. The game was nothing too exciting and finished 1 - 1. We were back in the centre of Amsterdam fairly quickly after the game so could spend another couple of hours in De Wildemaan until it closed at 1.

Thursday, 16th November 2006 Uneventful journey back - train to Schipol, 11:40 flight back to Heathrow, back to the car and en-route to Nottingham by about 12:15. Spent night at Roz's mum's.

Friday, 17th November 2006 Roz dropped me at Meadowhall on her way up to Newcastle. I spent an hour or so there before getting fed up and getting the bus into Sheffield. Managed to complete most of my Christmas shopping before getting another bus out to Hoggy's. Went to Butler's for a curry and then got pissed in Sheffield with Hoggy and Ed.

Saturday, 18th November 2006 Hoggy drove to Port Vale where we saw Swansea win 2 - 0. We were joined by Roz after the game and went for an Italian meal and a few pints in the Sportsman.

Sunday, 19th November 2006 Drove from Sheffield to Wheatley where we got to see Harvey for the final time before we head off to S.E.Asia. We went out for a couple of pints in the Railway before returning to the house for a curry.

Spent the remainder of the evening getting pissed on a selection of Belgium and German beers and got to bed at about 3am.
