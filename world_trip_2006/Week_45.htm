World Trip 2006/7 - Week 45

Monday, 16th April 2007 Did not do too much all day. Had breakfast on our balcony as it is not included in our room rate. Spent a few hours by the pool and an hour or so on the internet in the evening. We went out to Easy Divers but decided that we would probably not bother diving while we were here due to the prices and the fact that we have plenty of diving ahead of us in the next few weeks. Definitely in need of a day off alcohol and an early night!

Tuesday, 17th April 2007 Day spent mainly by the pool again. We had planned a session for the evening. We had a few drinks on the balcony again and ate at Landhaus but the session got interrupted by an argument and came to an early end!

Wednesday, 18th April 2007 Another quiet day spent sun bathing and not really doing a lot else. We did go for a walk in the evening to have a look at the area where Palm Resort used to be. The resort has been flattened and replaced with an upmarket hotel and is unrecognisable from when we first stayed there. The original Kokila restaurant has also been flattened and replaced by a massage parlour!

We walked back through Lamai photographing some more places that we used to visit regularly but have now been knocked down, closed down or simply changed for the worse.

Ate out at a restaurant on the beach - something that we have never done much in Lamai for some reason. Given the number of our old eating places that have closed or disappeared, this proved to be a far better option than eating Thai food in the centre of Lamai.

Thursday, 19th April 2007 A similar day to the previous ones! Apart from an hour or so when we went out to use the internet and buy books we remained by the pool most of the day.

Had our "leaving Lamai" session in the evening. Usual sort of thing - drinks on our balcony, Sang Som with our meal on the beach and a lot more beer than we had planned to consume at Landhaus.

Coming to Samui again has been a really good move and the Firefly flights have been ideal. Despite all the changes here, we have still enjoyed ourselves and would consider coming back again when on a two week holiday.

Friday, 20th April 2007 Not too hung-over for our journey back to Penang. An uneventful journey - taxi to the airport, flight left 20 minutes early at midday with a total of 9 passengers on board, taxi from airport to Bay View Hotel in Penang. Did not do a lot for the rest of the day except eat and make use of the internet in the room which we may be getting without having to pay.

Saturday, 21st April 2007 Spent most of the day trying, unsuccessfully to purchase a camera and underwater housing for my birthday. After our visit here last week, we thought that this would be a relatively easy task as we had enquired about what was available. We had chosen the camera and case that we wanted and had been given the price. However, when we went to buy the camera, the case was not in fact available. A few shops had underwater cases available but all for older models of camera that were no longer stocked. We had just about given up but after a search on the internet, we went and looked at another couple of shops. We found one that could sell us a camera that I was keen on and the case. We got as far as paying a deposit and walking to a bank to get out the money but when we returned to the shop, we were told that there was a problem with the case and there was no other one available. We got back our deposit and gave up for the day.

We decided that we would try again in KL on Monday. This would mean getting an early bus instead of flying but as we do not have much else to do all day this seemed a reasonable option. We rang a couple of places that said that they could help us but we will head to the shopping malls and see what is really available.

This took us most of the day and we were ready for a few drinks again. We had bought another bottle of Bacardi when we arrived from Thailand and we started on this before going out to eat. We ate in our usual curry house and then went for a couple of beers in a bar showing Spurs v Arsenal. We returned to the room and continued to drink the Bacardi while watching the football and listening to Gillette Soccer Saturday on the laptop (Swansea 2, Brighton 1).

Sunday, 22nd April 2007 Another day of not doing a lot apart from eating and using the internet. We had been thinking of visiting East Timor while in Indonesia but found out that the FO advise strongly against travel there at the moment. We started looking into other options but are still waiting to find out about live-aboard options so can not plan anything definite yet. We did book bus tickets to KL for tomorrow morning at 7:30. This will give us a few hours to look for cameras before we fly out to Tawau
