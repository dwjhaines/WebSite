World Trip 2006/7 - Week 50

Monday, 21st May 2007 A return to the more normal schedule of two dives in the morning and one at 3pm. The first dive was not as good as the others we have done here mainly due to the amount of dead coral around most of the dive site. The second was much better. For the third, Roz asked about going to see turtles and we were taken to a site that gives the best chance for seeing them. I managed to see seven turtles during the dive and Roz saw eight in total. As well as the turtles, during the day there were more sharks, bump-head parrot fish, nudibranchs, leaf scorpion fish, etc.

Another quiet evening and we were back in the room by just after 9.

Tuesday, 22nd May 2007 Our last day of diving at Bunaken. We did the first two morning dives on the Sulawesi mainland and returned to the same site as yesterday afternoon for the third. Only saw three turtles this time but the dives were all very good again. We had a couple of beers with the other guests before eating in the evening. There have not been that many other people staying here while we have been here, 4 - 10 other guests most of the time. We have liked just about everyone here and it has been a good group of people to dive with.

Two Fishes has been another excellent dive centre to dive with. The customer service would be hard to beat, the owners and staff very friendly, small dive groups and the diving itself has been excellent. Another place that we will hopefully come back to!

Wednesday, 23rd May 2007 We left Bunaken at 8:30 and were transferred to Two Fish's other resort at Lembeh. This involved a boat back to Sulawesi, just over an hours car journey and a 10 minute boat ride across to Lembeh. We arrived at the Lembeh resort just after 11 and went out for our first dive here 15 minutes later. The diving here is "muck diving" - very different to the diving at Bunaken. We dived at 11 and 3 and did a night dive at 6. All three dives were very good with loads of stuff that we had not seen before. Lots of new nudibranchs as well as all sorts of weird looking fish.

The resort is not as big or as developed as the one at Bunaken but is fine for a few nights. We ate with the only other couple who are staying here at the moment and were in bed by about 10:30.

Thursday, 24th May 2007 We got up at 2:45 to watch the Champions League final with all the male staff from the resort. We returned to bed two hours later to get a bit more sleep before starting another days diving.

Another three dives and almost four hours underwater again. All the dives were very similar but once again there was loads to see. Every time you think it is getting boring swimming over black sand, something else appears that we have never seen before. We saw a total of eight frog-fish during the day!

The Dutch couple that were here last night left after the second dive leaving us as the only guests in the resort. We had tea at 6:45 and after an hour or so looking at photos of the days diving with Vanus, our guide, we had an early night.

Friday, 25th May 2007 Two more dives and loads more strange creatures including the hairy frog fish. The first dive of the day was much the same as yesterdays with two sea horses and the hairy frog fish. The second was a more normal sort of dive along a coral slope but still with plenty to see including pygmy sea-horses.

This finished our diving for a few weeks. We could not do the afternoon dive as we fly tomorrow at 1:40 and need a 24 hours between diving and flying, particularly after the amount of repetitive dives we have done in the last ten days (29). Did not do a lot for the rest of the afternoon but did manage to dry off most of our dive equipment.

During the day we were joined at the resort by Barbara who we knew from Bunaken and Judi. We stayed up slightly later after eating but were still back in the room by 9:30.

Saturday, 26th May 2007 Left Lembeh at 8 and were taken back to the mainland on the dive boat. We then had an hour or so in the car to get back to Manado and were taken to the Two Fishes office to settle our bill. This involved a lot of messing around as both of our cash-point cards appear to have been blocked by Nationwide due to "irregular transactions". We were taken to a shopping mall for an hour or so before being collected and driven to the airport. Two more uneventful flights and we arrived back in Bali on time just before 6. We had booked three nights at Poppies and were met at the airport by one of their drivers although we ended up travelling to Kuta in a taxi.

The traffic on the way in to Kuta was terrible and the area we drove through looked horrendous - big new hotels and shopping malls everywhere - but when we were dropped off at Bemo Corner, not much of central Kuta seemed to have changed. We checked into Poppies which was very nice. Another really good room in pleasant gardens........ and free wifi!

We immediately went out to stock up on drinks and started on the alcohol after showers at about 7:30. We had a couple of drinks in the room before going out. The area where we used to do most of our drinking in Kuta was also largely unchanged. The Bali Asi which always had the cheapest beer was still there but with a different name. Our pizza restaurant was still open and still did very good pizzas. We had a few beers before during and after eating and were back in the room by about midnight. Do not really remember getting to bed!

Sunday, 27th May 2007 Did not do a lot all day apart from lying by the pool. We both had a free massage included in the price of the room and we had these by the pool at 4. Had a similar evening to yesterday with the same ending.
