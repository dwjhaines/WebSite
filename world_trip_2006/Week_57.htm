World Trip 2006/7 - Week 57

Tuesday, 10th July 2007 Left Kingsclere just before 7 and had a pretty good journey down to Dover. We were at Western Docks by 9:30 and had a wait of just over an hour for the ferry. The ferry left on time and by 1 we had arrived in Boulogne. We stopped at a couple of hyper-markets to stock up on wine and some French beer before continuing on to Ghent. We arrived in Ghent at 5ish and after a few problems finding our way into the centre and then parking the car, we checked into the same Ibis that we had been in exactly a year ago.

We headed out to Het Waterhuis where we had a couple of drinks before moving on to t Galgenhuisje. We  then went out to The Trappistenhuis where we stayed for the remainder of the evening. A remarkably similar night out to the 10th July last year!

Wednesday, 11th July 2007 We had breakfast and a walk around the city centre buying chocolates on the way back. After showers back at the hotel we headed off to start the days drinking at 2ish. We went off in the direction of the Hop Duvel stopping for a couple on the way. Spent most of the afternoon in the Hop Duvel before walking back to the centre. Spent the evening in a selection of bars in the centre and vaguely remember being in Het Waterhuis sometime around midnight.

Thursday, 12th July 2007 Checked out of the hotel at 11 and after managing to get out of the car park without too many problems, we drove to the Hop Duvel shop where we spent over €100 on beer. We added to the selection by stocking up on some of the less rare beers at a supermarket and then headed North to Ostend where we were to spend the last night of our world trip.

We went out for a walk around Ostend at 4ish - it was a bit like visiting a English sea-side resort in winter! We did however find an excellent bar with a range of about 280 beers so settled in there for the final session of the trip. We spent 3 - 4 hours in the same place drinking mainly stronger beers. We were both pissed by the time we left here and only managed one other bar before returning to the room by about 9. A very enjoyable way of spending our last night abroad.

Friday, 13th July 2007 Left the hotel at 10:30 for the drive back to Boulogne. Uneventful journey back and we were back in Dover by 2
