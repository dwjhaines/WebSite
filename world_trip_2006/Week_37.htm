World Trip 2006/7 - Week 37

Monday, 19th February 2007 We were picked up by Lanta Diver at 6:50 for another trip to Hin Muang and Hin Daeng. We left our luggage in the dive shop as we would be returning to Time for Lime when we got back.

We dived with Anjali again and Lars and Peter from Sweden. Both dives were good with better visibility than on previous trips to the same sites previously. We saw an unexpected turtle on the first dive as we battled with the current and there was a leopard shark at both sites (although I missed it at the first one as I was photographing the turtle!)

We were dropped at Time for Lime by about 7. The bungalow was nicer than we had thought it was yesterday and will be fine for a couple of nights. We tried to eat in the Time for Lime restaurant but it is closed on Mondays so we walked to Mr Wee's for another pizza instead.

Tuesday, 20th February 2007 Two dives back at Koh Bida Nok and Koh Bida Nai. Visibility was poor again but the dives were still OK. We were back at our room by about 4:30 and did not do a lot for the rest of the day. Spent an hour or so trying to connect to the free wi-fi that Time for Lime is supposed to have - without success. We had a couple of beers on our balcony and ate in the Time for Lime restaurant.

Wednesday, 21st February 2007 We checked out of Time for Lime and walked back to Golden Bay Resort where we had booked an air-con room for three nights. The room was not ready when we arrived so we spent an hour using the internet before checking in. Not a bad room for the price (B1300) apart from the lack of fridge!

Spent the rest of the day on the beach. We had a session again in the evening. The usual sort of thing - drinks on our balcony, a couple of bottles of Sang Som with our meal at Sunset and half of a third bottle that we do not remember anything about!

Thursday, 22nd February 2007 Did not really do a lot all day. Apart from getting up for breakfast, we did not leave the room until 12:30 due to the effects of last nights Sang Som. When we did get up, we walked into Saladan spending about 40 minutes on the internet before having lunch at Lanta Seafood. We booked our minibus tickets to Pak Bara (from where we get the ferry to Koh Lipe) for two days time, and then walked back to Golden Bay. Roz went to the beach for a couple of hours while I watched the Barcelona v Liverpool game on tele.

Ate at Sunset which is now only a couple of minutes walk from our bungalow and had an early night.

Friday, 23rd February 2007 Our final days diving before we leave Lanta - almost certainly for the last time on this trip. We had chosen to go to Koh Ha as it is a shorter day and had two very good dives again. We were diving on our own with Anjali although we did have one of the recently qualified DMs following us. Also on the boat was Jan, who we had dived with when on holiday here last year, and his daughter - both now dive master trainees..

On the first dive we managed to find the rhinopia (leaf scorpion fish type thing) that we have been looking for unsuccessfully twice previously. The visibility was very good for the rest of the dive which was quite shallow due to the rhinopia being down at about 29m. The second dive was also enjoyable at a part of Koh Ha #4 that we had not dived previously. We had a bit of a wait for transport back to the resort but were still there by 5ish.

We had not planned to drink too much tonight but as it was our last night and our last meal at Sunset, we still somehow managed to get through almost three bottles of Sang Som.

Saturday, 24th February 2007 We were picked up by a taxi at 7:45 and driven to our first minibus which left roughly on time just after 8. Not too upset to be leaving Lanta this time although seeing the Lanta Diver pick-up leave without us was a sad moment. We have stayed here 34 nights in total so it is probably time for moving on and doing something different. The journey continued to run smoothly at Trang where we transferred to another minibus with only about a 10 - 15 minuted wait. This minibus also left roughly on time and we were in Pak Bara by 12:45. We bought tickets for the 1:30 speed boat and had lunch before walking to the pier.

Here things started to go wrong. The boat, which was only supposed to take an hour did not leave until gone 2 and than seemed to do a tour of the islands en route to Lipe giving people time to get off the boat for pictures etc along the way. We got transferred to a long-tail boat at Lipe at about 4:30 and got onto the island about 15 minutes later.

The next problem was that the room that we had booked through a dive centre here was not available. The hotel had no record of the booking and neither the resort or the dive operator seemed overly bothered about doing anything about the problem. We left our luggage there while we walked along the beach trying to find an available room. We got something eventually, but at B1100 for a fan room, it seems very over priced. By this time we were both knackered and had to take the first room we found but will probably do something about moving tomorrow.

In the evening we went round a couple of other resorts - all cheaper than where we are staying but also all full. We will try again tomorrow as people are checking out for the early ferry. We asked at a dive centre (not the one that had cocked up our accommodation!) about diving in the area and will probably dive again on Monday. We ate at a restaurant on the beach and returned to our room to listen to the football on World Service (Yeovil 1, Swansea 0)

Sunday, 25th February 2007 After breakfast, we found a couple of rooms available a bit further along the same beach from where we were staying. We took the second room we saw at Daya Resort. It was the better of the two and slightly cheaper. At B400, it was a lot better value than last night.

Did not do a lot for the rest of the day. Spent most of the time reading either on the beach or on the balcony. We went for a walk round the island to see the other beaches which did not appear as nice as the one we are at. Booked ourselves on a three dive day trip for tomorrow. Ate at our resort's restaurant.
