World Trip 2006/7 - Week 56

Monday, 2nd July 2007 Got to the dive centre and were told that we would be diving down South again. We had already decided that we would not bother with another trip down South so we said we would have a day off instead. However, we were then given the option of joining a group of 18 divers from a club in Manchester who were heading for the Canyon and Bells - Blue Hole. This seemed a far better choice of sites and also meant that we could return to bed for an hour or so to sleep off our hangovers before diving. Once again, it was two good dives and a good day out.

Could not be bothered to walk into Dahab to eat so had a chicken balti at the Bedouin Moon and an early night.

Tuesday, 3rd July 2007 Dived at Eel Garden and Islands returning to the hotel in between. We dived with Mohammed (Big Mo as opposed to Little Mo who we dived with yesterday) and Jane who we dived with a couple of days ago. Eel Garden was a nice enough dive and Islands was very good as usual. The location of the dives gave us a bit more time for lying by the pool for a few hours during the day, something we have not been able to do much since we have been here.

Had a few beers and a takeaway in the evening but managed to avoid getting as pissed as we did a couple of nights ago.

Wednesday, 4th July 2007 Not a good start to the day as Roz received an e-mail from Divetours stating that our liveaboard left on the Sunday as it was supposed to and we would not be able to get a refund. Decided not to worry about it until we get home!

Our last two dives of the World Trip. We dived with Mark and were the only two customers so we had the choice of where to go. We chose to do the two dives at the Blue Hole again, the first from Bells and the second to the right of the Blue Hole. Both very nice dives again and a good way to finish. We ended the dive with over 480 logged dives meaning that I have logged 280 in the last year (Roz has 283 due to three more in Pulah Weh). Back at the hotel by 2 so plenty of time to wash our gear and spend a couple of hours by the pool.

Had a few beers again in the evening. Usual sort of thing - a few on the balcony, a few in Adam's Bar, three more with the meal at El Fanar and back to Adam's Bar before getting a taxi back to the hotel. Ended the night by finishing the end of the duty free on our balcony.

Thursday, 5th July 2007 A lazy morning spent mainly lying around in bed. We paid our bill at the dive centre and collected up all our dive gear and made it up to the pool by about midday. Picked up by minibus at 3 and driven to Sharm airport. Plane departed about an hour late at 7 and we were back in Gatwick by 11. Made it back to Kingsclere just after 1am.
