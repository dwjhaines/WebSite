World Trip 2006/7 - Week 51

Monday, 28th May 2007 Another day of not doing a lot. We had breakfast served in our room at 9ish. I made it to the pool for about 30 minutes before we went to lunch. We went to the beach for the first time since arriving here for the sunset at 5:30 but it was not too spectacular so went for a couple of beers instead.

We had a "Rijsttafel" included in our room package and we also had this served in our room. This was a selection of several dishes with rice and vegetables, etc and was an excellent meal. We went out for another beer after eating but Roz was getting sleepy by this time and we did not continue drinking.

Tuesday, 29th May 2007 Checked out of Poppies at 12. It has been an excellent place to stay for a few nights and we have both really enjoyed staying in Kuta again. It was an ideal way to end what has been a very good month in Indonesia! We were transferred to the airport for the 2:25 Air Asia flight to KL which left 15 minutes early. The flight took three hours and we arrived in KL just after 5. We had an onward flight from KL to Bangkok, also with Air Asia but we had to enter Malaysia, collect our luggage and check in again. While waiting to check in, we bought another SIM card and managed to sort out our debit cards with Nationwide. We had a couple of arguments about the weight of our check-in luggage and the amount of hand luggage that we were carrying but managed to get through to Bangkok without having to pay any excess baggage charges.

We left KL slightly late at 8:40 and got to Bangkok about 2 hours later at 9:45 local time. Arrival in Bangkok all went very smoothly. We were first through immigration, got our luggage quickly and due to the time of arrival, only spent about 30 minutes in the taxi getting to our hotel (Samran Place again).

Wednesday, 30th May 2007 Did not do a lot all day. We we went to the MBK for lunch and Roz went got her hair cut, but spent a lot of the day catching up on sleep and sorting out some of our diving photos. We had our final SE Asia session in the evening, starting in the room then continuing with a bottle of Sang Som at our usual restaurant at Siam Square. We had intended to have a relatively early night but bought a third bottle of Sang Som on the way back to the hotel and have no recollection of the end of the evening.

Thursday, 31st May 2007 We checked out of the hotel at 8ish without being too badly hung-over. We got a taxi to the airport, checked in and departed a few minutes late at 11:40. Uneventful 11 hour flight to Düsseldorf. where we arrived at 18:30 German time. We got the S-Bahn to the main station where we had booked a night at the Ibis.

After sorting out train tickets for tomorrow morning, we went out for a few beers. We did not make it as far as the old town but found the Paulaner beer hall where we had an excellent meal and 10 wheat beers between us. We stopped for a couple more on the way back to the hotel and had another in the hotel bar sometime after midnight. A very enjoyable night out!

Friday, 1st June 2007 Checked out of the hotel at 7:45, once again without being too badly hung-over. We caught a train to Cologne and from there another to the airport and checked in for the EasyJet flight back to Gatwick. Arrived back at Gatwick at 11, picked up a hire car and joined the traffic on the M23/M25. By 1, we had picked up the keys from the estate agent and were back in our house. Everything seemed to be pretty much as it was when we left it although within minutes of our arrival, we had complaints from next door about the tenants that had been in the house for 7 months!

We did not have time to do a lot before heading back up to London for the England v Brazil game at Wembley. We got the 15:05 train from Basingstoke and met up with Mark in a pub near Marylebone station. We were joined by Steve and Kim and all headed to the ground when the pub stopped serving at 6:30. Quite impressed with the ground especially from the inside.

After the game we headed for Waterloo as quick as we could and managed to get a train just after 11. Back in Kingsclere just after midnight.

Saturday, 2nd June 2007 We were both up at about 6:30 after less than 6 hours sleep. We left the house at 7ish and drove down to Swansea. We left the car at Cathy's and got the train from Neath to Cardiff, joining up with Dave and Melissa as well as Julie and niece on the train. We were joined by Regan in The Prince of Wales in Cardiff and stayed in there for a couple of hours before the Wales v Czech Republic game. We all returned there after the game and stayed until about 8 before getting the train back to Swansea (apart from Regan who left at 6:30).

Back in Swansea we visited The Adam & Eve and The Potter's Wheel before getting a taxi back to Cathy's. Do not remember a lot about the latter stages of the evening!

Sunday, 3rd June 2007 Left Swansea reasonably early and were back in Kingsclere by 12:30. Once again, we were only in the house for a few hours before heading out again, this time to Gatwick for our 7:30 flight to Tallinn. We got there pretty quickly so had three hours to kill before the flight. Uneventful flight and we arrived in Tallinn at 12:30. We were supposed to be collected by someone at the airport but no-one turned up. It took a few phone calls but eventually a taxi was sent to collect us and we were dropped at our apartment at 1ish.

The apartment we had booked was no longer available so we were upgraded to an apartment that could easily sleep 6 people. It also could not be much better located with superb views looking out on the main square in the old town. Eventually got to bed sometime after 2.
