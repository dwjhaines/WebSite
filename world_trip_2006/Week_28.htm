World Trip 2006/7 - Week 28

Monday, 18th December 2006 We had breakfast, spent an hour or so by the pool and checked out of the hotel just before 11. We carried all our stuff down to the pier and got on the 11:30 boat to Lanta. We arrived in Lanta just over an hour later and got a taxi to Kaw Kwang Resort. We checked into the bungalow that we had booked when we were here in March, just behind where the bungalow was that we checked out of exactly six years ago today. It was really good to be back and it meant that we could finally unpack the Christmas things that we have been carrying for the last few weeks. Hopefully this will be the last time that we will have to travel with so much luggage. We are booked in to Kaw Kwang for 15 nights - the longest we will have stayed in the same place since leaving home in June.

After unpacking everything, we walked into Saladan for lunch and also called into Lanta Divers to sort out our diving for the next couple of weeks. Again everything seemed to be very efficiently organised. We are booked to dive three days this week, again on Christmas day and three days the following week when Regan and Annette do their advanced course. We walked back to Kaw Kwang and spent what was left of the afternoon by the pool although it was not particularly sunny. I ran the length of the beach and back just after 5 while Roz sorted out the Christmas lights that we had bought in Chiang Mai.

We ate on the beach in the German run restaurant that we had eaten in several times when we were here in March. Another excellent meal and a few bottles of Chang (Leo does not seem to have made it to this part of Thailand).

Tuesday, 19th December 2006 We were collected from Kaw Kwang at 7:40 by Lanta Divers. We met Anjali, who we have dived with before at the dive centre and found that we were diving with her again as requested. We were also diving with a Dutch couple, Rose and Vincent, who are both dive masters.

It was a good days diving. We went to two dive sites just South of Phi Phi, Koh Bida Nok and Koh Bida Nai. Two good dives with a turtle being spotted fairly soon after the start of the first. I managed to stub my toe on the boat in between dives but other than that it was a very enjoyable start to our diving here. We were back in Lanta just after three so had time for an hour by the pool before the sun set.

My toe was quite bruised and swollen by the time we were back and it made walking difficult, so no running for a few days! We ate in the Kaw Kwang restaurant - excellent and very spicy food. We had a a couple of beers on our balcony before and after the meal as well as a couple of bottles of Chang while we ate. Quite light-headed by the time we went to bed.

Wednesday, 20th December 2006 We were supposed to be diving at Hin Daeng and Hin Muang today so were picked up from Kaw Kwang at 6:40. After an hour or so of the boat trip, there was a change of plan due to the strong winds and choppy sea and we diverted to Koh Ha instead. This did not turn out too badly as we had two pretty good dives at Koh Ha. The first dive featured a few caves, one of which you could surface inside of. It was a dive that we had done with Dive Zone six years ago. During this dive there were strong thermoclines that were not only very cold but also produced strong up currents. The second dive was easier and better with plenty to see. Dived with Anjali again and two English blokes. Due to the early start and the shorter than anticipated boat journey, we were back in Lanta soon after3.

We had an hour or so by the pool and started drinking at 6. Usual sort of Thailand session - a couple of beers on the balcony, a couple of bottles of Sang Som in a restaurant on the beach and another half bottle on the balcony that neither of us remember! My toe was still quite painful today. It was not too bad for diving but was not really up to walking into Saladan as we had planned. Still very bruised and swollen.

Thursday, 21st December 2006 We got up for breakfast but then returned to bed until after midday. Not too hung-over once we did get up. I walked into Saladan to get another couple of Christmas things leaving Roz to sort out the Christmas lights for our balcony. My toe was still bad but walking did not seem to make it any worse.

It was gone three by the time I was back. We had lunch in the hotel restaurant and stayed by the pool for the what was left of the afternoon. We both went into Saladan in the evening. We had a look round some of the shops and spent half an hour or so looking at the internet. Found out that there is a problem with fog in England at the moment causing flight cancellations. Regan and Annette have already had their flights changed and could have problems leaving England. Also looked at the web site of Lumba Lumba dive centre in Pulau Weh, Indonesia. We are considering doing DM training in February and Anjali has recommended this place to us. It all looked pretty good and this is probably the place we are most likely to go for.

We had planned to eat in one of the sea food restaurants in Saladan but ended up going for a pizza instead. After eating, we stocked up on beer and got a taxi back to Kaw Kwang.

Friday, 22nd December 2006 Our second aborted trip to Hin Daeng and Hin Muang. This time we got closer to the dive sites than two days ago and were only about 45 minutes away before the weather changed and we had to turn around. We dived the two sites at Koh Ha again. Both were good dives and it was another enjoyable day despite not getting to the better sites. We were back in Saladan by about 4:30 and we stayed for a couple of beers with some of the Lanta Diver staff and the two other divers that we had dived with.

We got a taxi back to Kaw Kwang at 6ish and continued drinking. We had a few more beers on the balcony and a half bottle of Sang Som with tea in Kaw Kwang restaurant. Ended up drinking more than we had planned! We discovered during the evening that Regan has made it successfully to Thailand and should be with us tomorrow.

Saturday, 23rd December 2006 We were woken at 7 by someone from Lanta Diver who thought that we should be diving today. We returned to sleep but were up and in breakfast reasonably early anyway. We walked into Saladan to use the internet and stock up with more alcohol for the next few sessions. Had a few hours by the pool either side of lunch.

Regan and Annette arrived in Lanta at about 4:30 and called to let us know they were here. We walked down to Summer House, where they are staying but only saw Regan due to Annette being in bed with a cold! We returned to Kaw Kwang for showers and then walked back to Summer House for a few beers on their balcony. We ate at the place on the beach that we had been to on Wednesday - good food and cheaper than the German place. We started on the Sang Som with the meal and got through a total of six bottles between us. Do not remember walking back at about 1:30.

Sunday, 24th December 2006 Apart from breakfast, I did not make it out of bed until midday again. We walked into Saladan to meet Regan and Annette for lunch at Lanta Seafood. We left them sorting out their diving with Lanta Diver and walked back for an hour or so by the pool. Regan and Annette made it to Kaw Kwang in the evening. We ate in our restaurant and had a couple of beers but no-one was really drinking and we were in bed before 10.
