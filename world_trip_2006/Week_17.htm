World Trip 2006/7 - Week 17

Monday, 2nd October 2006 Our last day of fun diving. We headed "Down South" again with JP and Hywel for a more relaxing days diving than when we were here with them last week. When we returned to Dahab, we waited an hour or so and then did a night dive back at the Lighthouse.

Dive 228: Golden Blocks
Dive was led by Hywel. Saw plenty of large fan coral. Went down deep at the start of the dive to look for "bigger" fish. Did not find much but saw a large potato grouper at about 10m.

Dive 229: Um Sid
Another site that we had not dived before. Plenty of spectacular coral again. Saw a couple of rays - a torpedo ray and a blue-spotted sting ray. Also saw a red sea walkman

Dive 230: Lighthouse
One of the best night dives we have ever done. Dived to the right of lighthouse over the training area where we did a lot of our rescue skills. Saw an octopus, four morays, a huge stone fish, dwarf lion fish, cuttle fish and loads of strange looking coral that we had not seen before.

By the time we had finished the night dive, washed our kit and had showers it was 8:30 so we could not be bothered to walk into Dahab. Instead, we joined Hywel, JP and Martin (who seems to be recovering) at the pool for a few beers and another chicken balti. We took a couple more bottles down to our balcony when everyone left and got to bed at about 1am.

Tuesday, 3rd October 2006 We checked out of the hotel at midday and walked into Dahab for lunch. We had to get about £600 of Egyptian pounds out to pay for our diving and then ate lunch at the restaurant by the Lighthouse again. We spent a couple of hours by the pool before having showers. We then spent an hour or so with JP and Hywel before our mini bus turned up to take us to Sharm. We were dropped off at the airport at about 7:30, checked in and waited for the 21:20 flight back to Gatwick. The plane left just about on time and after a couple of glasses of wine and the meal, we managed to sleep most of the way back.

Wednesday, 4th October 2006 Arrived in Gatwick at 1:50am and after collecting our luggage and waiting for the car, we were on our way an hour later. We drove directly to Llansteffan stopping once at a service station for breakfast and once at Tesco in Swansea to buy wine. Arrived in Llansteffan at about 7:30. Managed to avoid falling asleep during the day but did not really do a lot.

Thursday, 5th October 2006 Another day of not doing much. We were going to go into Carmarthen to do some shopping but could not be bothered due to the rain. We did make it in for a pizza in the evening with Mum, Jane and Ayla. Roz and I did not go back to Llansteffan from there instead going straight to Cathy's house in Swansea.

Friday, 6th October 2006 We got a taxi to the Commercial by 5ish, picking up Cathy from work on the way. We were joined by Dave and had a few pints before going to the Swansea v Tranmere game (0 - 0). We all returned to the Commercial after the game, stayed there until 11:30 and then went for a curry in Morriston. Got to bed by about 1:30.

Saturday, 7th October 2006 Got up before 8 for the three and a half hour drive to Macclesfield. After showers at Ed's, we got the train into Manchester arriving at The Knott at the same time as Steve and Kim. Stayed in the pub until about 4 and got taxis to Old Trafford. A pretty poor game and a disappointing result.

We had a couple of drinks on the way back to Manchester Piccadilly and got a relatively early train (9:32) back to Macclesfield. Ended up staying in a pub in Macc with Ed and Jim until gone 1.

Sunday, 8th October 2006 Left Ed's fairly late and eventually got to Nottingham at about 2pm. Went out for a meal with Roz's Mum and John and was in bed by 9:30 due to being totally knackered and having another early start tomorrow.
