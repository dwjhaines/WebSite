World Trip 2006/7 - Week 24

Monday, 20th November 2006 Drove down to Bognor (via Kingsclere) for lunch with Dad and Doris. From Bognor we went back up to Farnborough to stay at Alex and Andi's. A relatively early night but we still got through a few bottles of wine.

Tuesday, 21st November 2006 Left early to drive down to Swansea. We had to stop at the Thai consulate in Cardiff to attempt to get Thai visas as we plan to have two stays there of more than 30 days. This proved to be a remarkably easy task and we left the consulate with our visas about 10 minutes after turning up.

Stayed in Swansea with Cathy. Went to the Red Lion quiz with Dave. Ended up quite pissed again.

Wednesday, 22nd November 2006 Spent the morning recovering from the last few days and drove to Llansteffan after lunch. Ate at Jane's but had a relatively quiet evening.

Thursday, 23rd November 2006 Got the car MOT'd and completed the remainder of my Christmas shopping

Friday, 24th November 2006 Back down to Swansea to watch the Wolves v Sunderland game at Dave's followed by a curry.

Saturday, 25th November 2006 Christmas lunch at Mum's

Sunday, 26th November 2006 Took Ayla to her first football match (Swansea 0 - Bristol City 0) which she enjoyed more than we had expected. Said goodbye to Mum (who had waited in the car) Jane and Ayla and then rejoined Cathy in The Commercial. We had a few in there while watching Man United v Chelsea before getting a taxi to The Potter's Wheel. Several more in there before going for a curry. Don't really remember getting back to Cathy's
